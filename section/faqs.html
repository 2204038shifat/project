Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services and policies.

How do I apply for a new passport?

To apply for a new passport, please visit our Online Applications section under "For Citizens." You will find detailed instructions, required documents, and the application form there.

Where can I find information on local business regulations?

Information on local business regulations, permits, and licenses can be found in the Business Services section. You can also contact the Ministry of Commerce for specific inquiries.

How can I report a public service issue?

You can report public service issues through our Contact Us page, or by calling our general inquiry line during office hours.

What are the government's current environmental initiatives?

Details on our current environmental protection initiatives can be found in the About Us section, specifically under the Ministry of Environment (if applicable) or within relevant policy documents.
